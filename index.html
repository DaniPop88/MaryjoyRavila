🏬ABOUT US

Maryjoy Ravila Retail Mart has been a favorite shopping destination in Quezon City since 2010. We’re dedicated to offering a carefully curated selection of top-quality clothing, household goods, electronics, and accessories. Our mission is to make every purchase easy, affordable, and satisfying for our customers, while building relationships based on trust and service excellence.

Our store is conveniently located in Cubao, Quezon City, serving customers all over Metro Manila and across the Philippines. Every product we offer undergoes strict quality checks, and we’re committed to fast shipping, secure payments, and genuine customer care.

Our Mission: To provide affordable, high-quality products that make your daily life easier and more enjoyable.
Our Values:
  • Customer Satisfaction: We always put customers first.
  • Quality Assurance: Only products that meet our high standards.
  • Convenience: Fast delivery and secure payments.
